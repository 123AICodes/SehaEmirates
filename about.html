About Our Hospital

Dublin Health Services launched Seha Emirates Hospital in February 2012 as a boutique hospital providing global services in order to provide quality & patient-oriented Inpatient services to the group’s doctors. The three-story purpose-built 2,500 m2 Hospital was officially inaugurated under the patronage of H.H Sheikh Nahyan Bin Mubarak Al Nahyan, Minister of Culture, Youth, and Social Development. Dublin’s Health Services deployed vast resources to ensure that their principal project, Seha Emirates Hospital is up to world-class facility standard and provides exemplary levels of care, comfort, and opulence.

Proud to serve you in the following specialties: Bariatric Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Dentistry & Oral Surgery, E. N. T., Dietetics, General Surgery, Internal Medicine, Obs & Gyn, Orthopedics, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Pediatric Dentistry, General Medicine, Colorectal Clinic, Gastroenterology Diagnostic Imaging & Radiology Department in Seha Emirates Hospital provides patient-centric imaging services using state-of-the-art technology for the past 5 years, we have kept our physicians’ and patients’ satisfaction and comfort as our number 1 target. Innovative approaches to the design of the physical facilities, always with the patient’s experience in mind, have led to garnering the highest in patient satisfaction ratings. These improvements have included the addition of specialized staffing, and expedited scheduling, insurance approvals, report turnaround times, complete digital system, free WiFi & valet parking. To ensure that our patients receive the highest standard of care, our specialized staffing includes, for example, the Radiation Safety Officer, whose main responsibility is to lower, standardize, and monitor radiation dose at all of our radiological examinations.

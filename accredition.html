Awards & Accreditions

Joint Commission International (JCI)

Seha Emirates Hospital has successfully received accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI). The JCI Gold Seal of Approval is a globally recognized accreditation that reflects an organization’s commitment to best practices in quality and patient safety.

DoH’s Muashir assessment ranks

Seha Emirates Hospital has been ranked as ‘excellent’ by the DoH’s Muashir assessment, a quality index that evaluates standards of patient care from healthcare providers within the Emirate. Achieving a rating of ‘3 Diamonds’, our hospital was recognized for its timely and effective care for patients and staff satisfaction.

Malafi

Having a unified medical record provides three core benefits. Firstly, clinicians are given access to medical records when required, resulting in improved patient safety and treatment. Secondly, by avoiding unnecessary repetition, providers are able to significantly cut costs and finally, thanks to an aggregated volume of data, authorities will be able to better protect the population against contagious diseases.” Stated Dr. Mohammed Chabuk, Managing Director, on this occasion.

ABU DHABI - HEALTHCARE INFORMATION AND CYBER SECURITY STANDARD)

The Department of Health (DOH) intends to establish the Abu Dhabi Healthcare Information and Cyber Security (ADHICS) Standard, a strategic initiative in support of DOH’s vision and Federal/National mandates, endorsed by DOH’s Executive Committee. The provisions of this Standard are harmonized with industry and international expectations towards Information Security.

Abu Dhabi Occupational Safety and Health System

Abu Dhabi Government is committed to protect the health and safety of all workers and to improve the performance of workplaces to the best and highest levels in this area, in order to contribute to preventing and minimizing incidents and injuries, and occupational illnesses..
